Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TechItEz ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

TechItEz does not require user accounts, registration, or login. The app itself functions as a local reference calculator, and your calculations are processed entirely on your device.

However, we do collect limited, anonymized usage data through Google Analytics to help us understand how people use our app and improve it over time. This data does not personally identify you and includes:

  • Device type and browser information (e.g., "Chrome on Android")
  • General geographic location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Pages or screens viewed within the app
  • Time spent in the app and session frequency
  • Referring source (how you arrived at the app)

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, or any content you enter into the app's calculators.

Data Storage

All calculations performed within the app are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any external server.

Anonymized analytics data is transmitted to and stored by Google as part of Google Analytics. We do not store or retain any user data on our own servers.

Third Party Services

We use Google Analytics (a service provided by Google LLC) to collect anonymized usage statistics. Google Analytics may use cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze this information. For more details on how Google processes this data, please see Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

If you would like to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install Google's official opt-out browser add-on available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout, or use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting.

We do not use any advertising networks or sell any data to third parties.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the GDPR (Europe) or CCPA (California), including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Because we do not collect personally identifying information, most such requests will not apply. If you have questions about data Google Analytics may hold about you, please contact Google directly.

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for professional use by field technicians and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
